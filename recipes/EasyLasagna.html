Easy Lasagna

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • Beef: 1 pound lean ground beef
  • Sauce: 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • Cheese: 32 ounces cottage cheese / 3 cups mozzarella cheese / 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Eggs: 2 eggs
  • Lasagna Noodles: 9 lasagna noodles
  • Water: 1/2 cup of water
  • Spices: 2 tsp dried parsley / salt to tast / pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese,2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and Seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  4. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
