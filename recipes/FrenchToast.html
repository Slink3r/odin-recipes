Ingredients

  • White Bread: 6 thick sliced bread
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs
  • Milk: 2/3rd cups of milk
  • Vanilla and cinnamon: 1 tsp vanilla extract / 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon ( both optional )
  • Salt: Salt to taste
  • Butter: 1 tsp unsalted butter ( more as needed )

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and hear over medium-high heat
  4. Dunk bread in the mixture, soaking both sides
  5. Transfer to hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side
  6. Serve hot
